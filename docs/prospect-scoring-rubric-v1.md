# Prospect Scoring Rubric v1

Status: Locked for first San Jose landscaping list

Score each category from 1 to 5.
High total score means weaker site + stronger opportunity.

## Categories

### 1. Website Design Quality
- 1 = modern/professional
- 3 = acceptable but dated
- 5 = clearly weak/outdated

### 2. Mobile Experience
- 1 = mobile-friendly and easy to use
- 3 = usable but clunky
- 5 = poor mobile UX, cramped layout, hard taps, broken responsiveness

### 3. CTA Strength
- 1 = strong call/quote/request flow
- 3 = basic contact info only
- 5 = weak or unclear next step

### 4. Trust Signals
- 1 = strong reviews, testimonials, badges, licenses, before/after photos, proof
- 3 = some trust indicators but thin
- 5 = little or no proof

### 5. Branding / Visual Quality
- 1 = polished and credible
- 3 = average
- 5 = sloppy, generic, weak visuals

### 6. Lead Opportunity
- 1 = weak upside
- 3 = reasonable upside
- 5 = strong demand + obvious upside if conversion improves

## Priority Bands
- 22-30 = high-priority outreach
- 16-21 = medium-priority outreach
- 6-15 = low-priority outreach

## Required Capture Fields
- business name
- website
- phone
- email if available
- top 3 issues noticed
- likely offer fit
- total score
- outreach status

## Likely Offer Fit Rule
- salvageable site with clear conversion issues -> Quick Conversion Refresh
- badly outdated / structurally weak site -> Lead Gen Website Rebuild
- no current fit -> hold

## Reviewer Rule
Every scored prospect should include at least 2 concrete observations.
No score should exist without evidence.
